## Executive Summary

Artis Real Estate Investment Trust presents a **Ba/BB credit profile** characterized by elevated leverage (41.3% Debt/Gross Assets), weak interest coverage (1.81x NOI/Interest), and significant near-term refinancing risk with 38% of mortgages maturing in the remainder of 2025. The REIT is executing a portfolio rationalization strategy that has resulted in substantial revenue declines (-30.3% Q2 YoY) and a $26 million expected credit loss on preferred investments. However, operational performance remains resilient with 18 consecutive quarters of renewal rent growth (+3.6% Q2), improving occupancy (87.8%, 89.0% including commitments), and positive same-property NOI growth (+2.3% YTD). The REIT's conservative AFFO payout ratio (37.5%) provides modest financial flexibility, but fully drawn credit facilities and upcoming debt maturities constrain liquidity. The credit profile is transitioning as the REIT reshapes its portfolio, creating execution risk alongside potential for eventual deleveraging.

**Scorecard-Indicated Rating:** Ba2/BB (consistent with DBRS BB (high) rating)

**Key Credit Drivers:**
1. **Elevated leverage and weak coverage** - Debt/Assets of 41.3% and NOI/Interest coverage of 1.81x place the REIT in Ba/BB territory
2. **Significant near-term refinancing risk** - 38% of mortgages mature in 2025 with credit facilities fully drawn
3. **Resilient operational performance** - Positive same-property NOI growth and consistent renewal rent increases demonstrate underlying asset quality
4. **Portfolio transition execution risk** - Large credit losses and revenue declines reflect active disposition strategy
