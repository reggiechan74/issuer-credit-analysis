## Executive Summary

**Scorecard-Indicated Rating:** Ba2 / Ba3 (analysis-derived)

Allied Properties REIT faces **elevated credit risk** driven by unsustainable distribution coverage, high leverage, and significant market stress. The AFFO payout ratio of 166.7% indicates distributions exceed cash generation, while the distribution cut prediction model assigns a 59.9% probability of a near-term cut (HIGH risk).
