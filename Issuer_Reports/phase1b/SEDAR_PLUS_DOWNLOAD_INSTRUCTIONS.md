## General Instructions

### How to Download from SEDAR+

1. **Navigate to SEDAR+:** https://www.sedarplus.ca/
2. **Search for REIT:**
   - Click "Search for a company" or use search bar
   - Enter ticker symbol (e.g., "HR-UN.TO") or company name
   - Select the correct issuer from results
3. **Filter by Document Type:**
   - Select "Financial statements" for quarterly reports
   - Look for "Interim financial statements" or "Annual financial statements"
4. **Find the Correct Quarter:**
   - Check filing date matches observation quarter
   - For Q4: Look for "Annual financial statements"
   - For Q1-Q3: Look for "Interim financial statements"
5. **Download Required Documents:**
   - **Financial Statements** (PDF): Consolidated financial statements with notes
   - **MD&A** (PDF): Management's Discussion and Analysis
6. **Save with Standard Naming:**
   - Format: `{ticker}_{quarter}_{year}_statements.pdf`
   - Format: `{ticker}_{quarter}_{year}_mda.pdf`
   - Example: `HR-UN_Q4_2022_statements.pdf`, `HR-UN_Q4_2022_mda.pdf`
7. **Save Location:** `Issuer_Reports/phase1b/pdfs/{observation_number}/`
