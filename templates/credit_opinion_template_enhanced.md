This section analyzes the issuer's recurring economic earnings (FFO/AFFO), sustainable cash flow (ACFO), and adjusted free cash flow (AFCF) metrics, providing comprehensive earnings-based and cash flow-based perspectives on distribution sustainability and capital markets reliance.

- **FFO (Funds From Operations)**: Earnings metric starting from IFRS Net Income, adjusted for non-cash items per REALPAC (January 2022)

## Methodology and Limitations

### Rating Methodology

This credit analysis applies a 5-factor scorecard methodology consistent with Moody's, DBRS, and S&P rating frameworks for real estate companies:

1. **Scale (5%):** Based on gross asset size
2. **Business Profile (25%):** Asset quality and market position
3. **Access to Capital (20%):** Capital markets access and refinancing capability
4. **Leverage & Coverage (35%):** Debt ratios and interest coverage
5. **Financial Policy (15%):** Distribution policy and growth strategy

### Data Sources

- **Phase 3 Calculated Metrics:** Quantitative financial data (strong evidence)
- **Phase 4 Credit Analysis:** Qualitative assessment (moderate evidence)
- **Rating Methodology Standards:** Industry frameworks (strong evidence)

### Evidence Quality

- **Strong Evidence:** Quantitative metrics from financial statements
- **Moderate Evidence:** Qualitative assessments and scorecard application
- **Limited Evidence:** Business profile, access to capital (requires additional research)

### Key Limitations

1. **Single Point-in-Time Analysis:** Based on {{REPORT_PERIOD}} data only

3. **No Management Guidance:** Forward-looking assumptions not validated by management

7. **Forward-Looking Uncertainty:** Scenario analysis based on assumptions that may not materialize
